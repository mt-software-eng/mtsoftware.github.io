Refund Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026 · MT Software

We want you to be happy with Maytay Finance. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Web Purchases (Windows & Linux) — via Lemon Squeezy

Subscriptions purchased through our website are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our payment partner.

  • 14-day money-back guarantee: If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, contact us within 14 days of payment and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.
  • After 14 days: Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We will always try to find a fair solution.
  • Cancellations: You can cancel your subscription at any time. You will retain access to Premium features until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused time after the 14-day window.

To request a refund for a web purchase, email us at support@mtsoftware.app with your order number and we will process it promptly.

App Store Purchases (iOS, iPad, Mac)

Subscriptions purchased through the Apple App Store are subject to Apple's refund policy. MT Software does not have access to process these refunds directly. Please request a refund through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Google Play Purchases (Android)

Subscriptions purchased through Google Play are subject to Google's refund policy. Please request a refund through Google Play's support or at play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.

Free Tier

The free version of Maytay Finance has no charges, so no refunds apply.

Contact

For any refund request or question, reach us at support@mtsoftware.app. We typically respond within 1 business day.
