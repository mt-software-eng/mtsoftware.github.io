Privacy Policy

Maytay Finance — developed by MT Software
Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Data Storage

All data you enter in Maytay Finance (accounts, transactions, budgets, receipts) is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

MT Software does not collect, store, or access any personal or financial information. We have no servers that receive your data.

3. Camera and Photo Library

The app requests access to your camera and photo library only to let you attach receipt images to transactions. These images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

4. Analytics

Maytay Finance does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Questions? Email us at support@mtsoftware.app
