Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026  ·  MT Software  ·  Maytay Finance

Maytay Finance is built on a simple principle: your financial data is yours. It never leaves your device.

1. Data Storage

All data you enter in Maytay Finance — accounts, transactions, budgets, receipts, and goals — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. No data is ever transmitted to any server or third party.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

MT Software does not collect, store, or have access to any personal or financial information. We have no servers that receive your data. We cannot see what you enter in the app because it never leaves your device.

3. Camera and Photo Library

The app may request access to your camera and photo library to let you attach receipt images to transactions. These images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

4. Analytics

Maytay Finance does not include any analytics, crash reporting, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

7. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@mtsoftware.app.
